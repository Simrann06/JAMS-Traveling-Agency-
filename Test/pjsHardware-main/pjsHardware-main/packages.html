Ghost Town Expedition

Embark on a spectral journey through the haunted alleyways and timeless streets of St. Augustine, America's oldest city. Our ghost tour reveals tales of lost souls, ancient curses, and eerie encounters from its 450-year history. As twilight falls, follow lantern-lit paths to the city's most paranormally active sites, from the shadows of Castillo de San Marcos to the tragic grounds of the old hospital. Your expert guide will share spine-tingling stories and historical hauntings that have made St. Augustine a must-visit for ghost enthusiasts. Brace yourself for an unforgettable night where history comes alive, and the past's restless spirits await. Join us... if you're brave enough!
